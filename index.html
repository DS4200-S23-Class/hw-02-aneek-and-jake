Task Abstraction

The high level actions of this graph would be present because the creater is showing specifically how high tempetures have risen and so a specific point is trying to be made. The mid-level action in visualization is browse becuase we know the location of the point we are looking for but we are looking to see the points aroud the graph to see the message it is trying to convey. The low level actions of the graph is identify because we are looking for a specific group of points that are trying to convey recent high tempetures.

The high level actions of this visualization is discover because there is no message being directly converyed but rather people are able to explore different components of the data. The mid-level action is explore becuase there is no specific place to look and it is up to the user in order to find the meaning in the visualization. The low level action is compare because we are looking at two different charts and the main goal is to see the difference between them. The target of this chart is to compare how unusually high tempetures are in one city compared to a second.
